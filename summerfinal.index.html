About

Located on top of a hill in Allegany County, New York, Woolly Star Farm is the home to Icelandic Sheep that are raised for fiber, pelts and meat. Icelandic Sheep are a primitive breed that have been bred in the United States for about 30 years and are considered a multipurpose breed. The colors of their wool (white, black and moorit) as well as their interesting patterns including spotting make this breed unique! Beyond our sheep, we grow grow fruits, flowers and vegetables. For more information contact woollystarfarm@gmail.com
